What is Art?

This site is dedicated to a bunch of random stuff, be it photography, 3D modelling, other forms of digital art and traditional art. Yeah basically a bunch of art. Basically everything. Everything can considered be called art right? Music is art. 3D modelling is art. Programming is art. Literature is art. Architecture is art. Painting is art. Drawing is art. Art is art. Making windows could be an art. Nature is art. Trees are art, you know what trees I'm refering to ;). Sculpting is art. Brick laying is art. Playing games on your phone isn't art. Making games is art. Overcharging for your games is art. EA is a pretty good artist. Making people disappear is art. Making wallpaper is art. Mathematics is art. Putting up with other people is art. Blazing, I mean burning down trees is art. Flowers are art. Putting a piece of IKEA furniture together is art. Photography is art. Cinematography is art. Acting is art. Running a soulless corporation is art. Dancing is art. Science is art, ok not really. Religion is art, treading on thin ice saying that one. Cooking is art. Baking is art. Playing sports is art. Watching sports is not art, no it's not YOUR team, it's the team YOU SUPPORT. Cutting stuff out is a precise art. Building a computer is art. Decorating a room is art. Decorating a house is art. Decorating a funeral is art. Decorating a wedding is art. Okay you get it, there's lots of art. Everywhere. So yes, this site is essentially dedicated to art. My art.
